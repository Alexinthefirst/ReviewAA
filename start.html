Welcome to ReviewAA


  ReviewAA allows businesses to examine their reviews on a per product basis. It will aggregate all reviews from across the web and provide them in an easy to read dashboard. This dashboard provides key areas of complaint that are seen in the reviews, as well as potentially fake reviews that are bringing down the score. Our company will then reach out to these reviewers to attempt to get them to change their review, or reach out to the platform to get them to remove fake reviews.


  Our software has the strength to act against harmful reviews. Using publicly available systems, such as fake review reporting, and contacting reviewers, we can positively affect reviews of a product. Using algorithms to analyze reviews to get the key points out of them, we can relay this information to the user and help provide them with a clear path forward for their product.
